lillian li, about

Lillian Li is the author of the novel Number One Chinese Restaurant, which was an NPR Best Book of 2018, and longlisted for the Women’s Prize and the Center for Fiction’s First Novel Prize. Her work has been published in the New York Times, Granta, One Story, Bon Appetit, Travel & Leisure, The Guardian, and Jezebel.


She received her MFA in fiction from the University of Michigan and her BA from Princeton. Originally from the D.C. metro area, she lives in Ann Arbor.
